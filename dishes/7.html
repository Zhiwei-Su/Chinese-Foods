Braised Pork Rice Lu Rou Fan

卤肉饭



Introduction

Braised Pork Rice is a staple food in Taiwan, well known for its budget friendly price and spices used to braise the pork.


Geography and Origin

Braised pork rice is a traditional snack in the Jiangnan area. The specialty of braised pork rice is meat sauce and gravy, which are the key part of the production. The methods and characteristics of braised pork rice are different in Tainan, Taichung, and Taipei. There are many kinds of food and snacks in Taiwan, but the most famous one is braised pork rice. Braised pork rice, also known as Lu pork rice, is one of Taiwanese snacks.



Ingredients

The main ingredients are

  • 100 grams of pork
  • 400 grams of pork filling
  • 7 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 5 fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 or 2 eggs(depends on your preference)
  • Appropriate amount of oil
  • 10ml cooking wine
  • 15ml light soy sauce
  • 15ml braised soy sauce
  • 5g sugar
  • a little rapeseed

Hainan Chicken Rice

海南鷄飯



Introduction

Hainan Chicken Rice is made with rice cooked with Chicken broth. It is a traditional dish in Hainan Island, the Southernmost tip of China. It is also very popular in places like Singapore and Malaysia.


Geography and Origin

Hainan Chicken Rice originated from Hainan Island, the Southernmost Island of China.

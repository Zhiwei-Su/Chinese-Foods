Rou Gu Cha

肉骨茶



Introduction

Bak Kut Teh, also known as bone soup, is usually made from parts like pork ribs, tail bones and pepper. It is a traditional dish from Fujian. It is very popular in Southern China and Singapore.


Geography and Origin

Bak Kut Teh is a way of eating both flesh and blood. The Hokkien: Bah-Kut-Teh, the flavor is well-known at home and abroad. Bak Kut Teh is divided into Singapore’s Teochew Pie and Malaysia’s Fujian Pie. Teochew Bak Kut Teh has a heavier pepper flavor, while Fujian Bak Kut Teh has a stronger medicinal flavor. Ingredients

The main ingredients are

  • 500g pork ribs
  • 6 rapeseed hearts
  • 8 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 10 garlic cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) white peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons (10g) Chinese wolfberry
  • 2 star anise
  • 3 grams of licorice
  • 2 slices of tangerine peel
  • 2 dried longans
  • 10ml of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of salt (5g)

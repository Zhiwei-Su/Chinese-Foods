Spicy & Sour Soup with Beef

香辣酸湯肥牛



Introduction

This dish is part of the Sichuanese Cuisine, which is AKA Kingdom of Spice. You will love this if you love Spicy food.

Sour soup: The content of calcium and phosphorus is relatively high, and the content in the following several kinds of sour soup is relatively stable. The minerals in the human body mainly exist in the bones, which play a role in maintaining the rigidity of the bones. Therefore, the rich calcium, phosphorus, iron, etc. of Miao sour soup play an important role in maintaining the excitability of nerves and muscles and maintaining the acid-base balance of the body. No wonder the local people in Guizhou say that "do not eat sour for three days, walk and jump", there is a certain scientific basis.

Ingredients

The main ingredients are

  • Beef 400G
  • Enoki Mushroom 300G
  • Coriander
  • Millet Spicy and Xiaohang Pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of Lantern Chili Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of Wild Mountain Pepper Pickled Pepper Water
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1 small piece of concentrated chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of rice wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Recipe is translated from: XiaChuFang


Cooking Methods

Here are the methods:

  • Boil water in the pot, turn off the heat when the water is turned on, put the washed enoki mushrooms in the water and drain them.
  • Boil the water again, turn off the heat after the water is turned on, put the beef rolls in the water, and change the color. The reason why you want to turn off the fire and fly the water is to keep the beef rolls fresh and tender. If you are not careful, the beef rolls are old and drained.
  • Put the wok on the heat, pour oil, add ginger, garlic slices, and chopped green onion and stir fry.
  • After frying the aroma, add the western red pan and stir fry, pay attention to the fry for a longer time, until the thick juice comes out.
  • At this time, add Kaili red sour soup and stir fry a few more times.
  • Add a soup bowl of water.
  • After boiling, add a little sugar, salt, seasoning, and then add wild sansho pepper, you can pour some wild sansho pepper juice in, but don't put it too early, because too long cooking time will evaporate the sourness, if it feels not sour enough , You can add more white vinegar.
  • After cooking for a while, put in enoki mushrooms and beef, put in MSG, and turn off the heat immediately.
  • Put the wok on the heat, pour the oil, add the peppercorns and stir flagrantly, in order not to affect the appearance, throw away the fried peppercorns, pour the pepper oil on the beef with sour soup and sprinkle it with coriander.



Recipe is translated from: XiaChuFang
